Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about project management services, processes, and how I can help your team succeed.

What methodologies do you use for project management?

I primarily use Agile and Scrum methodologies, adapting the framework based on project requirements. I combine these with best practices in stakeholder management, risk mitigation, and user-centered design to ensure successful project delivery.

How do you integrate UI/UX design into project management?

I embed design thinking and user research from the project initiation phase. Using Figma and design systems, I ensure that UX considerations are part of every milestone, leading to products that are not only on-time and on-budget but also user-delightful.

What's your approach to managing remote or distributed teams?

I leverage project management tools, clear communication protocols, and regular sync meetings to keep distributed teams aligned. I focus on transparency, documentation, and asynchronous collaboration to ensure all team members stay connected and productive regardless of location.

How do you handle scope changes and project risks?

I implement robust change management processes and maintain a risk register from project start. Any scope changes are evaluated for impact on timeline, budget, and quality. I proactively communicate risks to stakeholders and develop mitigation strategies early.

Can you help with React development and technical architecture?

Yes! I have hands-on experience with React development and can bridge the gap between design and development. I can advise on technical architecture, component design, and best practices to ensure clean, scalable, and maintainable code.

What industries have you worked with?

I've successfully delivered projects across diverse industries including fintech, healthcare, e-commerce, SaaS, and enterprise software. This breadth of experience allows me to bring best practices and innovative solutions to any industry vertical.
